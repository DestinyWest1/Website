Shih Tzu Breed

Shih Tzu's are one of the most common lap dog breeds around. Bred to be companions, these dogs are a happy, playful and affectionate breed until the very end. Shih Tzu's are an ancient breed originating near china. Their Chinese heritage affects quite a bit of their characteristics. Like many Shih Tzu's Simba's tail curves and lands on his back which in Chinese culture shows respects and pride. Since Shih Tzu's usually weigh between 9-16 pounds, they are the perfect size for a lap dog. They are great dogs for families with small children because of their playful nature and are huge fans of indoor space because they are very prone to becoming overheated. Air conditioning and the cool breeze of a fan are their best friends.

Training Simba

Simba is an extremely playful and spunky dog but he can also very stubborn. We needed to start training him very early on so he would grow up with good habits. We started with things like:
- Walking with a leash
- Walking beside me and not in front
- Telling me when he needed to go outside
- Sleeping in his cage
